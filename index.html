Jeremi Jackson

Born into a lower middle class family in the bustling city of Birmingham, AL in the 1990s, Jeremi Jackson always new that there was more to be discovered. After enduring many trials and seeing the death of his family, the now adult Jeremi seeks to forge a new path. In an endeavor to make a new life for himself, he has found himself under the tutelage of a new master, TrueCoder John Ward. What will become of Jeremi as he struggles just to maintain in his training? Stay tuned...
